The Good Print Test

VCCP Spain · Worten · El Sol & Cannes Lions 2019

The challenge: humanizing the technology of a tech retailer.

Giving a new use to a printer test page.

Worten is a technology retail store on a mission to "humanize" technology, focusing on all the life that any product can offer. That was the line of communication we worked on from VCCP.

La idea

Giving a new use to a print page

We took something as simple, rudimentary and "lifeless" as printer test pages and turned them into a tool that could save lives. Together with the National Transplant Organisation, we created a software that turned this page into a ready-to-send application to become an organ donor. Beyond the form, each page included the real story of a patient waiting for a transplant.
