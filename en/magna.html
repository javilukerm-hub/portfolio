Sé infiel a ti mismo

After Madrid · San Miguel Magna

The challenge: making Magna by San Miguel the beer of Gen Z.

Celebrate the contradictions of a generation disloyal to itself.

San Miguel wanted to reposition Magna — a beer crafted like a premium, but meant to be enjoyed any day — as the beer for Gen Z. A contradiction in the product that was crying out for a contradiction in the campaign.

The idea

A campaign disloyal to what campaigns usually are

Building on Magna's contradictory nature, we spoke to one of the most contradictory generations in history: Gen Z. With the brand concept "Be disloyal to yourself" we created a campaign disloyal to what campaigns usually are. Through spots, visuals and activations, we invited Gen Z to celebrate their contradictions with a beer crafted to be disloyal to itself. Gay and catholic, ecologist but addicted to fast fashion, feminist but a reggaeton lover... Gen Z found a safe place to enjoy their double sides with a Magna.
