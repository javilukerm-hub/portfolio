About

Words to memorize, words hypnotize, words make my mouth exercise.

For 6 years I worked in advertising at VCCP Spain and After Madrid, with clients like Worten, Vans, Verti and Turespaña, among others. I took a break to continue growing as a professional, completing a UX Writing postgraduate degree at Shifta. I currently work as a Copywriter Specialist in the marketing department at Magnific.

I've done creative, copywriting, translation and UX writing work. I love being part of projects with deep user research that helps generate insights and solutions to real problems, iterating and testing each step with the end user.

I also enjoy building solid campaigns with strong concepts behind them. The kind that make you go "I wish I'd done that". These days I combine creative strategy with AI tools like Magnific and Claude, staying on top of trends in automation and creation without losing the human touch that helps brands differentiate themselves.

While you read this I've carefully curated a Spotify playlist to keep you company while you browse. Or to take a break from looking at portfolios.
