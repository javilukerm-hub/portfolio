Estate al Lory

VCCP · Worten · Black Friday 2018

The challenge: help Worten gain notoriety during Black Friday.

Team up with someone who carries "Money" in his name.

Worten, one of Spain's biggest technology retail chains, needed an idea to stand out from the competition during 2018 Black Friday — the most aggressive sales period of the year, where every brand shouts the same thing — and to grow its sales.

The idea

A song instead of a promo

To communicate the "money" customers could save at Worten, we teamed up with rapper Lory Money. Together we created "Estate al Lory": a song and music video that became the centrepiece of the most viral Black Friday campaign of 2018. And what mattered most: it helped Worten grow sales by 23% and take the lead in the year's biggest sales moment.

The saga

When it works, you do it again

Success turned the campaign into a strategy. During the 2019 Spanish elections we teamed up with Lory again for "Lory President", keeping Worten as the one to watch in tech retail. And for Black Friday 2019, we ran the formula once more with Glitch Gyals.
